What is a diffusion model?

Forward SDE $$d\mathbf{X}_t = -\tfrac{1}{2}\beta(t)\,\mathbf{X}_t\,dt + \sqrt{\beta(t)}\,dW_t$$
Reverse SDE $$d\mathbf{X}_t = \Bigl[-\tfrac{1}{2}\beta(t)\,\mathbf{X}_t - \beta(t)\,\nabla\!\log p_t(\mathbf{X}_t)\Bigr]dt + \sqrt{\beta(t)}\,d\overline{W}_t$$
Score From Noise $$\nabla_x \log p_t(x) = -\frac{1}{\sigma_t}\,\E[\epsilon \mid \mathbf{X}_t=x] \approx -\frac{1}{\sigma_t}\,\epsilon_\theta(x,t)$$
Predict Noise $$\min_\theta \E_{t,\epsilon}\,\bigl\|\epsilon_\theta(\mathbf{X}_t,t)-\epsilon\bigr\|^2$$

Classic references: Sohl-Dickstein et al. (2015), Song & Ermon (2019), Ho et al. (2020), Song et al. (2021), Karras et al. (2022).
