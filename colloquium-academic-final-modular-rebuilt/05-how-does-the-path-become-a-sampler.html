Sampling via ODE

Compatible vector field Suppose there is a vector field $u_t:\mathbb{R}^d \to \mathbb{R}^d$ satisfying $$\partial_t p_t + \nabla\!\cdot\!(u_t p_t)=0.$$ Then sample $x_0 \sim p_0$, solve $\dot{x}_t = u_t(x_t)$, and let $\Phi_t$ be the flow map.
Then $(\Phi_t)_\# p_0 = p_t$.
