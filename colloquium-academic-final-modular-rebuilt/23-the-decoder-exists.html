Terminal stage: convergence of ODE trajectories at $t=1$

Theorem 5.3 — Wang et al. 2025 Assume $p$ has finite second moment, $\supp(p)$ has positive reach $\tau_\Omega>0$, and a local density lower bound. Then $$\Psi_1(z):=\lim_{\sigma\to 0}\Psi_\sigma(z)\text{ exists a.e.},\quad (\Psi_1)_\# p_{\mathrm{prior}}=p,\quad \|\Psi_1(z)-\Psi_\sigma(z)\|=O(\sigma^{\zeta/2}).$$
