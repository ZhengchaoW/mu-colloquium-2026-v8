What training sees of Xσ

The fuzzy blue ball is the wrong mental model: in high dimension, Gaussian mass concentrates on a shell.

Start with the familiar blue ball cartoon, but explicitly say it is the wrong way to picture a high-dimensional Gaussian. Then let the shell plot do most of the work: the shell concentration should be the big visual on the page. Keep the spoken point simple: training sees shell supervision around atoms, not a filled Gaussian ball.
