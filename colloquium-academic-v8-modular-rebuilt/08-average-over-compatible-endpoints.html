Average over compatible endpoints

Posterior average
$$u_t(y) = \E[u_t(y \mid X) \mid X_t = y]$$
$$= \E[\dot{\alpha}_t X + \dot{\beta}_t Z \mid X_t = y]$$
Continuity equation
\((u_t,p_t)\) satisfies the continuity equation.
$$\partial_t p_t + \nabla\!\cdot\!(u_t p_t)=0$$
Closed form
$$u_t(y)=\frac{\dot{\beta}_t}{\beta_t}y+\frac{\dot{\alpha}_t\beta_t-\alpha_t\dot{\beta}_t}{\beta_t}\,\E[X \mid X_t=y]$$
