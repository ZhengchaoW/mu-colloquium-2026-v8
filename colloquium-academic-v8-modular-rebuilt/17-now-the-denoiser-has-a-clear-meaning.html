Now the denoiser has a clear meaning

Denoiser $$m_\sigma(x)=\mathbb{E}\!\left[X\,\middle|\,X+\sigma Z=x\right]$$
Sampling ODE ($\sigma:\sigma_{\max}\to 0$) $$\frac{dx_\sigma}{d\sigma}=-\frac{1}{\sigma}\bigl(m_\sigma(x_\sigma)-x_\sigma\bigr)$$
Training $$\min_\theta\;\mathbb{E}_{\sigma,X,Z}\,\bigl\|m_\sigma^\theta(X+\sigma Z)-X\bigr\|^2$$
The whole Part I problem is now contained in $m_\sigma$ and the $\sigma$-ODE. Next slide: the two geometric properties we need.
