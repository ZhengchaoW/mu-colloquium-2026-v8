Theorem 4.9 (informal)

Let \(S_i := \mathrm{Shell}_\sigma(x_i)\) be the Gaussian shell around training point \(x_i\).
If \(S_i \cap S_j = \emptyset\) for \(i \neq j\), then the shell-only denoising objective has infinitely many global minimizers: each sends \(S_i \to x_i\), and is arbitrary off \(\bigcup_i S_i\).
The transition sentence is important: the empirical optimum may be scary here, but the training support is too thin for this stage alone to be the formation mechanism.
